Sketched Face Shape Guide

Oval
Balanced proportions with gently rounded features. Length is about 1.5 times the width.
Round
Soft curves with full cheeks. Width and height are nearly equal with rounded jawline.
Square
Strong angular jawline with broad forehead. Width and height are similar with defined edges.
Heart
Wide forehead and cheekbones with narrow, pointed chin. Creates an inverted triangle.
Diamond
Narrow forehead and jaw with wide, prominent cheekbones. Creates a diamond shape.
Triangle
Narrow forehead with wider jawline. Also called pear-shaped face.
