1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use FrameFinder, we may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Email address (if you choose to subscribe to our newsletter or contact us)
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Usage Information: How you interact with our service, pages visited, time spent

1.2 Biometric Data

Our core service involves analyzing facial images to determine face shape. This processing includes:

  • Facial Images: Photos you upload or capture using your device's camera
  • Facial Landmarks: Mathematical coordinates extracted from your facial features
  • Face Measurements: Ratios and dimensions calculated for face shape analysis
  • Analysis Results: Your determined face shape and confidence scores
Critical Privacy Protection: Your facial images are processed entirely in your browser using client-side AI technology. Images are never uploaded to our servers or stored remotely.

3. Data Processing and Storage

3.1 Client-Side Processing

FrameFinder uses advanced client-side AI technology to ensure maximum privacy protection:

  • Local Processing: All face analysis occurs directly in your browser
  • No Server Upload: Your facial images never leave your device
  • Temporary Storage: Images are only held in browser memory during analysis
  • Automatic Deletion: Images are automatically deleted when you navigate away or refresh
